What Are the Sustainable Development Goals?

The SDGs are 17 global goals set by the United Nations to promote peace, prosperity, and sustainability for all by 2030.

Focus SDGs

  • SDG 13: Climate Action – Taking urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts.
  • SDG 14: Life Below Water – Conserving and sustainably using oceans and marine resources.
  • SDG 15: Life on Land – Protecting forests, reducing deforestation, and preserving biodiversity.
