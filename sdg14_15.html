🌊 SDG 14 - Life Below Water

Oceans cover 70% of the Earth's surface and provide oxygen, food, and climate regulation. However, pollution, overfishing, and climate change are endangering marine life and damaging fragile ecosystems.

⚠️ Challenges Facing Our Oceans

  • 🏭 Pollution: 8 million tons of plastic enter the ocean each year.
  • 🎣 Overfishing: Unsustainable fishing practices are depleting fish stocks.
  • 🌊 Rising Temperatures: Warmer waters are killing coral reefs.
  • 💧 Water Acidification: Increased CO2 is making oceans more acidic.

💙 How Can We Help?

  • 🚯 Reduce Plastic Use: Use reusable bags, bottles, and straws.
  • 🐟 Eat Sustainable Seafood: Choose fish from responsible sources.
  • 🏖️ Protect Marine Life: Participate in beach cleanups and avoid littering.
  • 📢 Support Ocean Conservation: Advocate for stronger ocean protection policies.

🌿 SDG 15 - Life on Land

Forests, wetlands, and grasslands are essential for biodiversity and human well-being. However, deforestation, habitat destruction, and climate change are pushing many species to extinction.

⚠️ Environmental Threats

  • 🔥 Deforestation: 10 million hectares of forests are lost every year.
  • 🐘 Wildlife Extinction: 1 million species are at risk due to habitat destruction.
  • 🏜️ Desertification: Land is turning into desert due to over-farming.
  • 🏭 Pollution: Air, water, and soil pollution harm ecosystems.

💚 How Can We Help?

  • 🌳 Plant Trees: Support reforestation efforts.
  • 🐾 Protect Endangered Species: Avoid products made from threatened wildlife.
  • 🚜 Support Sustainable Farming: Choose organic and locally grown food.
  • ♻️ Reduce Paper Waste: Use digital documents instead of printing unnecessarily.
